## Question 1
**Topic**: Simplificación de radicales (índice par)

**Question**: Simplifique: \(\sqrt{98a^6b^5}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
\sqrt{98a^6b^5} &= \sqrt{49\cdot 2\cdot a^6\cdot b^4\cdot b}\\
&= 7a^3b^2\sqrt{2b}
\end{align*}
\]
(Asumiendo \(a,b\geq 0\))

**Answer**: \(7a^3b^2\sqrt{2b}\)

---

## Question 2
**Topic**: Simplificación de radicales (índice par)

**Question**: Simplifique: \(\sqrt{50x^2}+\sqrt{32x^2}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
\sqrt{50x^2}+\sqrt{32x^2} &= 5|x|\sqrt{2}+4|x|\sqrt{2}\\
&= 9|x|\sqrt{2}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(9|x|\sqrt{2}\)

## Question 3
**Topic**: Simplificación de radicales (índice impar)

**Question**: Simplifique: \(\sqrt[3]{-128x^7y^4}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
\sqrt[3]{-128x^7y^4} &= \sqrt[3]{-64\cdot 2\cdot x^6\cdot x\cdot y^3\cdot y}\\
&= -4x^2y\sqrt[3]{2xy}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(-4x^2y\sqrt[3]{2xy}\)

## Question 7
**Topic**: Racionalización (denominador monomial)

**Question**: Racionalice: \(\dfrac{10}{\sqrt{5}}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\frac{10}{\sqrt{5}}=\frac{10}{\sqrt{5}}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{5}}{\sqrt{5}}=\frac{10\sqrt{5}}{5}=2\sqrt{5}
\]

**Answer**: \(2\sqrt{5}\)

## Question 8
**Topic**: Racionalización (denominador monomial)

**Question**: Racionalice: \(\dfrac{6}{\sqrt[3]{9}}\)

**Solution**:
Para eliminar \(\sqrt[3]{9}=\sqrt[3]{3^2}\), necesitamos \(\sqrt[3]{3^3}\):
\[
\begin{align*}
\frac{6}{\sqrt[3]{9}} &= \frac{6}{\sqrt[3]{9}}\cdot\frac{\sqrt[3]{3}}{\sqrt[3]{3}}\\
&= \frac{6\sqrt[3]{3}}{\sqrt[3]{27}}\\
&= \frac{6\sqrt[3]{3}}{3}\\
&= 2\sqrt[3]{3}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(2\sqrt[3]{3}\)

## Question 9
**Topic**: Racionalización (denominador binomial)

**Question**: Racionalice: \(\dfrac{5}{4-\sqrt{2}}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
\frac{5}{4-\sqrt{2}} &= \frac{5}{4-\sqrt{2}}\cdot\frac{4+\sqrt{2}}{4+\sqrt{2}}\\
&= \frac{5(4+\sqrt{2})}{16-2}\\
&= \frac{20+5\sqrt{2}}{14}\\
&= \frac{10}{7}+\frac{5\sqrt{2}}{14}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(\frac{10}{7}+\frac{5\sqrt{2}}{14}\) or \(\frac{20+5\sqrt{2}}{14}\)

## Question 10
**Topic**: Racionalización (denominador binomial)

**Question**: Racionalice: \(\dfrac{3}{2+\sqrt{5}}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
\frac{3}{2+\sqrt{5}} &= \frac{3}{2+\sqrt{5}}\cdot\frac{2-\sqrt{5}}{2-\sqrt{5}}\\
&= \frac{3(2-\sqrt{5})}{4-5}\\
&= \frac{6-3\sqrt{5}}{-1}\\
&= -6+3\sqrt{5}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(-6+3\sqrt{5}\) or \(3\sqrt{5}-6\)

## Question 11
**Topic**: Suma y resta de radicales

**Question**: Simplifique: \(2\sqrt{12}+\sqrt{27}-5\sqrt{3}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
2\sqrt{12}+\sqrt{27}-5\sqrt{3} &= 2(2\sqrt{3})+3\sqrt{3}-5\sqrt{3}\\
&= 4\sqrt{3}+3\sqrt{3}-5\sqrt{3}\\
&= 2\sqrt{3}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(2\sqrt{3}\)

## Question 13

## Question 14
**Topic**: Notación exponencial

**Question**: Simplifique: \(\dfrac{x^{3/2}\,x^{1/2}}{x^{5/2}}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
\frac{x^{3/2}\,x^{1/2}}{x^{5/2}} &= x^{(3/2+1/2-5/2)}\\
&= x^{-1/2}\\
&= \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\) or \(x^{-1/2}\)

## Question 15
**Topic**: Problemas mixtos

**Question**: Simplifique: \(\sqrt[3]{a^5}\)

**Solution**:
\[
\begin{align*}
\sqrt[3]{a^5} &= a^{5/3}\\
&= a^{3/3+2/3}\\
&= a\cdot a^{2/3}\\
&= a\sqrt[3]{a^2}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(a\sqrt[3]{a^2}\) or \(a^{5/3}\)

## Question 17
**Topic**: Dominio de funciones con radicales

**Question**: Determine el dominio de \(g(x)=\sqrt{5-2x}\)

**Solution**:
Para que \(\sqrt{5-2x}\) sea real:
\[
\begin{align*}
5-2x &\geq 0\\
-2x &\geq -5\\
x &\leq \frac{5}{2}
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \((-\infty,\frac{5}{2}]\) or \(x \leq \frac{5}{2}\)

**Answer**: \((-\infty,\infty)\) or \(\mathbb{R}\)

## Question 19
**Topic**: Práctica adicional - Multiplicación de conjugados

**Question**: Simplifique: \((\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2})(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2})\)

**Solution**:
Usando la diferencia de cuadrados \((a+b)(a-b)=a^2-b^2\):
\[
\begin{align*}
(\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2})(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2}) &= (\sqrt{3})^2-(\sqrt{2})^2\\
&= 3-2\\
&= 1
\end{align*}
\]

**Answer**: \(1\)

## Question 20
**Topic**: Práctica adicional - Dominio con radicales

**Question**: Determine el dominio de \(f(x)=\sqrt{x^2-9}\)

**Solution**:
Para que \(\sqrt{x^2-9}\) sea real:
\[
x^2-9 \geq 0
\]
\[
x^2 \geq 9
\]
Esto ocurre cuando \(x \leq -3\) o \(x \geq 3\).

**Answer**: \((-\infty,-3] \cup [3,\infty)\) or \(x \leq -3\) o \(x \geq 3\)
